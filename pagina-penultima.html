One of the most important
aspects of our work is our
partnership with local farmers,
where we favor and strengthen
the growth of their communities.
We call them Naturanic
Guardians, a vital part of our
cycle of Good.
From the start, The Natural Organic Co. have adopted a
business model based on an intense partnership with
local producers, thus favoring and valuing the growth
of these regions
20 years transforming realities through the preservation and recovery of the Atlantic Forest, one of the most important biomes in the world. This is the mission of Instituto Auá, an NGO based on the commitment to sustainable practices through agroecology, reforestation and solidarity economy. The Institute carries out several initiatives focused on offering prominence to family and organic agriculture, through financial training, assistance in the commercialization, preservation and cultivation of products native to the Atlantic Forest.
